EFinance Privacy Policy



Last Updated: 04-28-2023

At EFinance, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our customers and users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about our users and their use of our accounting management software, EFinance.

Information we collect

At EFinance, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our customers and users. When you use EFinance, we may collect the following types of information:

a. Personal Information: We may collect personal information, such as your name, email address, phone number, and billing information, when you register for an account or use certain features of our software.

b. Usage Information: We may collect usage information, such as the pages you visit and the features you use, to help us understand how you interact with EFinance.

c. Device Information: We may collect device information, such as your IP address, browser type, and operating system, to help us provide and improve EFinance.

How we use information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

a. To provide and improve EFinance: We use the information we collect to provide and improve our accounting management software, including personalizing your experience and providing customer support.

b. To communicate with you: We may use your information to communicate with you about EFinance, including sending you updates and information about your account.

c. To comply with legal obligations: We may use your information to comply with applicable laws and regulations.

How we share information

We may share the information we collect with the following parties:

a. Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who help us provide and improve EFinance.

b. Business Transfers: If we sell or transfer all or a portion of our business, we may transfer the information we collect to the acquiring company.

c. Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to a subpoena or court order.

How we protect information

We take reasonable measures to protect the information we collect, including using encryption and secure servers to store your information.

Your choices

You may choose to limit or opt-out of certain types of information collection, such as marketing emails. You may also access and update your personal information by logging into your account.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website.

Contact us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at efinanceofficial@gmail.com.

Thank you for using EFinance!
